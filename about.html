Meet Jingxiu.

Hi I'm Jingxiu, an aspiring UX designer/researcher. 👋 I'm from Singapore, but I'm currently based in London to pursue an MSc in Human-Computer Interaction from University College London (UCL).

Before getting into UX, I completed a BSc Psychology degree at UCL, where I learnt about human cognition and behaviour and was heavily involved in psychology research. After taking a Human-Computer Interaction module, I fell in love with the field as I realised that UX lies at the intersection of my interests in cognitive psychology, design and technology. I hope to apply my extensive knowledge of human psychology and my strong research skills to create human-centred products that provide meaningful user experiences.

In my free time, I enjoy learning and improving myself, be it reading a new book, meddling with the newest design software or refining my programming skills. I also love volunteering, playing music and travelling!
