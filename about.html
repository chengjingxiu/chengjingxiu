Meet Jingxiu.

My name is Jingxiu and I'm a self-taught designer with a background in psychology. I'm Singaporean, but I am currently living in London to pursue an MSc in Human-Computer Interaction at University College London (UCL).

I fell in love with UX when I realised that it perfectly captures my interests in cognitive psychology, design and technology. I enjoy placing the user at the centre of every digital product, and using my understanding of human cognition and behaviour to create meaningful user experiences.

In my free time, I enjoy learning and improving myself, be it reading a new book, meddling with the newest design software or picking up a new programming language. I also love volunteering, playing music and travelling!
